# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and receiving feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or insulting comments
- Trolling, threats, or repeated disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainers directly.
